Popular Destinations

Kashi

Kashi is considered one of the holiest cities in India and is home to many important Hindu temples. Hindus believe that dying in Kashi will lead to salvation and freedom from the cycle of rebirth.

Kedarnath

Kedarnath is in the Himalayas, at an altitude of 3,586 meters, near the head of the Mandakini River. The town is surrounded by snow-capped peaks, including the Kedarnath Mountain.

Jagannath Puri

The temple is one of the oldest Hindu temples and is considered the most sacred shrine in India. It is also part of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites for Hindus.

Trekking

The Himalayas are a popular destination for trekking, where you can see high peaks, plateaus, and forests, and experience local culture. Some popular trekking regions include Kasol Kheerganga, Triund in Mcleodganj, and Annapurna in Ladakh.
